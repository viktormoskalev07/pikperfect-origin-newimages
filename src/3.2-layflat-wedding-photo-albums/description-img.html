What is a Layflat Wedding Photo Book?

Developed on genuine photo paper, our layflat wedding books use one continuous sheet of photo paper for each double page creating gorgeous uninterrupted panoramic spreads.

These layflat photo books can have up to 120 pages (60 spreads), which gives you plenty of room to include all your favourite wedding photos.

Layflat Book Pages

Developed on Fuji album paper, our flat lay wedding photo books feature seamless, double-page spreads to showcase your most stunning photographs.

With incomparable brilliance and outstanding resolution thanks to this silver halide paper, your photos will have natural-looking skin tones and bold, bright colours that will last a lifetime. Select between Pearl Matte and Gloss pages to perfectly reflect your special day.
