Archival Photo Papers

PikPerfect flush mount photo books are printed on archival photo papers which are then mounted on rigid white board, resulting in beautiful seamless spreads.

Choose between Fuji Crystal Archive DP II photo paper pearl matte or glossy, which offer outstanding colours, natural skin tones and incredible definition. If you prefer a more subtle matte look with low sheen, our acid-free Fine Art Albums (smooth or eggshell finish) offer a lush tactility, archival quality and timeless appeal.
