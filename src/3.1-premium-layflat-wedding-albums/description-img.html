What makes these albums extra special?

Also called a Flush Mount Wedding Album, these high-end wedding albums are printed on professional photo paper which is then "mounted" on thick cardboard inserts. Their thick pages lay perfectly flat with a virtually invisible crease between the pages, making for stunning panoramic spreads to display your most important wedding photos.

Printed on archival photo papers and professionally bound, these luxury wedding albums are made to stand the test of time, in style.

Luxurious Album Covers

Browse our range of premium album covers to create an exquisitely finished flush mount wedding album. If you prefer a more customised look, our laser-etched titles enable your wedding logo to be added to the cover design. Custom embossed logos and titles are also available on request.
