Laser Etched Titles

Laser engraved photo album covers offer the flexibility to use virtually any design and font combination to create your custom photo book cover design. With laser etching, the heat from the laser is used to create the desired pattern on the linen or leather photo album cover by removing a thin layer from the fabric top to create the desired photo book cover design.
