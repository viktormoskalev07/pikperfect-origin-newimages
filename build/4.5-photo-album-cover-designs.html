Laser Etched Titles

Laser engraved photo album covers offer the flexibility to use virtually any design and font combination to create your custom photo book cover design. With laser etching, the heat from the laser is used to create the desired pattern on the linen or leather photo album cover by removing a thin layer from the fabric top to create the desired photo book cover design.

Cameo Windows

Adding a cameo window or photo indent window allows you to enjoy the benefits of a luxurious leather or fabric album cover, while being able to also personalize it with your favourite photo. Cameo windows are available in either square or rectangular, and can be positioned on the cover as shown.

Glass Photo Albums

Increasingly popular with newlyweds, our acrylic glass photo album features a glass album cover, paired with your choice of leather or fabric wrapped stylishly around the back. These sophisticated photo books allow you to admire your favourite images while maintaining the luxurious touch of a fabric photo album cover.

Imagewrap Photo Covers

A favourite for every occasion, our Imagewrap hardcover photo books wrap your photos across the front cover, back cover, and spine. Printed with full-bleed on a single sheet of photo paper which is laminated with a matte finish, these contemporary covers can be designed with any combination of photos and text.

More about our photo album title options

Can I choose the exact position of my custom photo album title?

Yes. Our photo albums & books are debossed by hand, which allows us to place your album title exactly as youd like it to appear.

Can I combine a cameo window with a custom album title?

Absolutely! But please keep in mind that the title cannot be placed right on the edge of the album cover, so we will recommend a design that allows for this.

Which covers can have a laser etched or embossed title?

Laser etched or embossed photo album titles are possible on all fabric photo album covers, including genuine leather, vegan leather and linen covers. These are not available on Imagewrap or glass photo album covers.

Do I have to choose one of your title designs or can I use my own?

We offer a range of different laser etched or custom embossed photo book titles style, but if you have some specific wedding album title ideas, please share these with us and well be happy to incorporate them into your cover design.
