More about our Custom Photo Books

How many photos can I include in my book?

Available in five different sizes, these large photo books can have up to 244 pages which can hold up to approximately 1,000 photos.

Do the pages lay flat?

No, these books are PUR bound so handle like a normal book. If you’re looking for layflat pages, we would recommend choosing either a layflat photo book or a premium layflat photo album.

Can I add text?

Yes, of course. As every photo book is custom designed and includes the option to add as much text as you like.

How much does a custom photobook cost?

The cost varies based on size, cover type and number of pages you choose. You’ll find the price will automatically update when you choose the options to start making your own photobook.

How long does production and shipping take?

The average production time is 6-8 business days. Delivery times depend on the destination and mode of shipping, you can find more details on the photobook prices page.
